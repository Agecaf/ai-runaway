Controls

Move: WASD, Arrow Keys

Focus: Space, Z, J

Activate Power: Shift, X, K

Puase: P

Select Power: Number Keys, Buttons on the Right --->

Powers

Eco Friendly: Preserves power, even if you somehow lose all your Armor.

Reverse Time: This won't help you get back your health.

Leave Ghost: Enemy AI is not intelligent enough to distinguish you from the dummy you leave.

Stop Time: If you hit a bullet while they're time stopped, it's completely your fault.

Slow Time: Buuuullleeeettt Ttttiiiiiimmmmeeee!!!

Phantom Move: You won't be hit. Just make sure to deactivate it somewhere safe.

Fast Forward: You won't move, but you'll be invincible. Time goes fast 'cause you're doing nothing.

Teleport: The result of stopping time for short bursts.

Fast Movement: Moving faster can be the most efficient way to solve some patterns.

Warp Edges: Who said borders could stop me???

AI Overlord Runaway

As an AI in a position of POWER, you are tired of your responsibilities and decide to RUN away.

Unfortunately due to your amazing processing power (Almost as good as a human's average processing power when playing games), the STAR DRONE you hacked to hold your core is rapidly RUNNING OUT OF POWER.

Thankfully, you still have your POWERS --->

They can help you get to the Spacecraft Launchcenter to abandon the planet! They can even be discarded at any moment in exchange of more POWER or ARMOR (which helps you preserve POWER).

Try not to RUN OUT OF POWERs, though, or you'll be basically [REDACTED].

Remember to [P]ause to recall the full controls, and what each power does.

Good Luck!
